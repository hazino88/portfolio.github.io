About me

Front End Developer

Hello and welcome to my page, I am Haroon Hashmi and I am a front end web developer based in London. I have always had an interest or hobby in computer gaming since a child playing on the desktop with classics such as Doom, Duke nukem and championship manager to name a few.

I have worked in the customer service and food industry for a very long time, I felt I needed to do something more challenging in my life. Having met people from the tech industry through my life and making friends, I became interested in being a developer and began with the advice of friends using code academy and other platforms. The visual aspects of how website are built and made to look pulled me towards front end aspect of design.

I am now currently enrolled on a self funded web developers program with a training company called Just IT. Coming from no experience, it has helped me to learn more and to gain confidence in what I do and I would love to continue learning and bettering my knowledge and skills throughout my career.
